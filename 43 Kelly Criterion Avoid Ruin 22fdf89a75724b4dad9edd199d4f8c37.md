# **凯利公式：避免破产**

## 不要孤注一掷

**Nivi：** 我们来聊聊凯利公式（Kelly criterion）。

**Naval：** 凯利公式是对一个简单概念的数学化表达。这个概念很简单：不要孤注一掷。不要坐牢。不要把全部筹码押在一次豪赌上。每次下注都要谨慎，不要把老本输个精光。

The Kelly criterion is a popularized mathematical formulation of a simple concept. The simple concept is: Don't risk everything. Stay out of jail. Don't bet everything on one big gamble. Be careful how much you bet each time, so you don't lose the whole kitty.

如果你是个赌徒，凯利公式会从数学上告诉你，即使你有优势，每一手该押注多少——因为即便有优势，你也可能输。假设你的胜率是51比49。每个赌徒都明白，不能把所有筹码押在这个51比49的优势上——因为你可能输掉一切，再也没有机会回归均值。

If you're a gambler, the Kelly criterion mathematically formulates how much you should wager per hand, even if you have an edge — because even when you have an edge, you can still lose. Let's say you have a 51-to-49 edge. Every gambler knows not to bet the whole kitty on that 51-to-49 edge — because you could lose everything and won't get to come back to the average.

纳西姆·塔勒布（Nassim Taleb）曾大力阐述"遍历性"（ergodicity）这一概念——这个听起来高深的词，其实说的是一件很简单的事：**100个人的平均结果，与同一个人重复100次后的平均结果，并不相同。**

Nassim Taleb famously talks about ergodicity, which is a fancy word for a simple concept: What is true for 100 people on average isn't the same as one person averaging that same thing 100 times.

最直观的理解方式是俄罗斯轮盘赌。假设六个人各玩一次，每位幸存者可得10亿美元。结果一人死亡，五人各得10亿美元。再对比一下：一个人用同一把枪独自连玩六次——这个人永远不可能成为亿万富翁，他只会死去，分文不剩。所以，冒险——尤其是当平均值通过大规模人群计算得出时——并不总是理性的。

The easiest way to see that is with Russian roulette. Say six people play Russian roulette one time each, and each winner gets $1 billion. One person ends up dead and five people get $1 billion. Compare that to one person playing Russian roulette six times with the same gun. They are never going to end up a billionaire — they will be dead and worth zero. So risk-taking — especially when the averages are calculated across large populations — is not always rational.

## 毁掉你的名誉，等同于归零

凯利公式帮你规避破产风险。现代商业中，人们走向破产的头号原因，并非押注过重，而是走捷径、做不道德甚至违法的事。**穿着橙色囚服身陷囹圄，或声誉尽毁，与被清零没有任何区别——所以，永远不要做那些事。**

The Kelly criterion helps you avoid ruin. The number one way people get ruined in modern business is not by betting too much; it's by cutting corners and doing unethical or downright illegal things. Ending up in an orange jumpsuit in prison or ruining your reputation is the same as getting wiped to zero — so never do those things.
